Online Escape Room

What better way to quench your boredom during quarantine than to play some Online Escape Rooms?

I am currently collaborating with Escape It to create a virtual escape room. This room will feature 3D graphics and gameplay using mostly C#/Unity. The rooms are entirely online and can be accessed by multiple players at once. The rooms can be played either solo or with a group, and can be played on different difficulty levels.
